2. "rejects duplicate codigo within same plantation" — create two subgroups with same codigo, expect second to fail with duplicate error.
3. "finalizes a subgroup (activa -> finalizada)" — create subgroup, call `finalizeSubGroup(id)`. Verify estado='finalizada' in db.
4. "does not finalize already-finalized subgroup" — finalize twice, expect error or no-op on second call.
5. "deletes a subgroup and its trees" — create subgroup with 3 trees, call `deleteSubGroup(id)`. Verify subgroup and trees removed.

Import: `createSubGroup`, `finalizeSubGroup`, `deleteSubGroup` from `SubGroupRepository`.

3. "getSyncableSubGroups excludes subgroups from other plantations" — create subgroups in 2 plantations, verify filtering.

Import: `getSyncableSubGroups`, `markSubGroupAsSincronizada` (or equivalent) from `SubGroupRepository`.
